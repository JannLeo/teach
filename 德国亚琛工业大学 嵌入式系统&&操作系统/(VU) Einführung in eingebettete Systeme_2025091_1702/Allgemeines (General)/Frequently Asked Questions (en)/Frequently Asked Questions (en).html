Exam information

(Zuletzt bearbeitet: Mittwoch, 23. April 2025, 11:05)

Dates: July 31st, 2025 9:00 am | September 16th, 2025 12:00 pm

Type: Digital Dynexite Exam

Location: In presence at the RWTH ZuseLab

Contents: All content presented in the lecture slides / exercises may be queried (chapter 6 is the last chapter, no more will follow).

Frage:

Where can questions be asked?

(Zuletzt bearbeitet: Dienstag, 8. April 2025, 11:37)
Antwort:

Questions that arise during a lecture / exercise: These questions are welcome to be asked to the instructor directly during or after the course. 

Questions about organizational things (e.g. regarding dates, locations, regulations, technical problems etc.): Please post these questions in the Organizational Questions forum.

Questions about lecture & exercise contents (e.g. regarding lecture / exercise contents, task descriptions etc.): Please post these questions in the Content Questions forum.

Question that do not belong to either category: Please post these questions in the Organizational Questions forum.

Question that you do not wish to ask in the forum (e.g. if it contains personal information): These questions are welcome to be emailed to us at  emsy@embedded.rwth-aachen.de.

Frage:

Which languages do we offer?

(Zuletzt bearbeitet: Dienstag, 8. April 2025, 11:37)
Antwort:

Lecture: The live lecture is in German and English, with English slides. Recordings of the last years are published in the Moodle learning room.

Exercise: The live exercise is in German and English, with English task sheets and slides. Recordings of the last years will be published in the Moodle learning room.

Exam: At the start of the exam you can choose between German and English problem definitions.

Communication: You can communicate with us in both German and English.
